# Business Analytics — Target Company Research

Project repository for structured target-company research used in business analytics and M&A screening.

## Purpose
- Provide a repeatable process, data artifacts, and reports to shortlist target companies.

## Structure
- `methodology.md` — research approach and steps
- `qualification_criteria.md` — inclusion/exclusion rules and scoring
- `data_validation.md` — validation checks and quality rules
- `research_limitations.md` — known limits and mitigations
- `source_references.md` — citation log
- `reports/` — final written reports
- `raw_research/` — raw notes and signals
- `outputs/` — final Excel deliverables
- `assets/` — logos, screenshots, figures

## How to use
1. Review `methodology.md` and `qualification_criteria.md`.
2. Populate `raw_research/` notes during data collection.
3. Run analysis and export results to `outputs/master_company_database.xlsx`.
4. Fill `reports/` with narrative findings.
