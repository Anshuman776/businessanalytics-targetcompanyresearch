# Research Limitations

## Known Limitations
- Public data may be incomplete or outdated.
- Private company financials are estimates and may be inaccurate.
- Biases in sampling and source availability.

## Impact
- Some candidates may be mis-scored due to missing or conflicting data.

## Mitigations
- Maintain a manual review step for high-impact candidates.
- Use multiple sources and timestamp all data pulls.
