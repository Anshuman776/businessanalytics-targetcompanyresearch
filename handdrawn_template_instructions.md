# Hand-drawn Diagram — Instructions

1. Print `handdrawn_template.svg` on A4 paper (landscape or portrait).  
2. Use a black pen to trace the boxes and labels, then add counts for each source bucket (e.g., 10,000).  
3. Add your weekly timeline notes below the funnel and mark tools at each stage.  
4. Write your name and date at the bottom.  
5. Take a clear photo of the hand-drawn sheet and upload it to the Internshala chat as `yourname_1000_plan.jpg`.

Tips: use bold strokes, ensure text is legible, and include arrows showing flow between stages. The diagram must be hand-drawn (no digital diagrams accepted).
