# Qualification Criteria

## Purpose
Define clear inclusion and exclusion rules for target selection.

## Inclusion Criteria
- Industry fit: primary industry codes or keywords.
- Revenue / ARR range.
- Employee count range.
- Growth signals: hiring, funding, product launches.
- Strategic fit: complementary products or markets.

## Exclusion Criteria
- Legal or regulatory issues.
- Direct company conflicts or sanctions.
- Unsuitable business model or extreme risk.

## Scoring Rubric
- Assign points per criterion (e.g., 0–5). Sum to produce a ranking score.
- Example weights: Strategic fit 30%, Growth signals 25%, Financials 25%, Operational fit 20%.

## Thresholds
- Shortlist if score ≥ 75; borderline if 50–74; reject if < 50.
