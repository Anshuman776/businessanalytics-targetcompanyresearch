# Methodology

## Objective
Describe the step-by-step research approach used to identify and evaluate potential target companies.

## Scope
- Geography, industry verticals, company sizes, timeframe.

## Data Sources
- Public company registries, Crunchbase, LinkedIn, company websites, job boards, press releases.

## Approach
1. Define qualification criteria.
2. Run broad market scans and scrape target lists.
3. Collect raw notes into `raw_research/`.
4. Validate and clean data (`data_validation.md`).
5. Score companies against criteria.
6. Produce shortlist and reports.

## Tools & Scripts
- List any scripts, data pipelines, or tools used (Python, Excel, SQL, APIs).

## Deliverables
- `outputs/master_company_database.xlsx`, shortlist, reports in `reports/`.
