# Business Analytics — Target Company Research Assignment

## Project Overview

This project contains a large-scale target company research and qualification exercise conducted for recruitment and business analytics evaluation purposes.

The objective was to identify, evaluate, classify, and prioritize companies that align with the assignment’s target criteria using structured market intelligence research.

The final deliverables include:
- Research on 100+ companies
- Qualification categorization
- Shortlisted target companies
- Rejected and borderline companies
- Analytical reasoning
- Confidence scoring
- Research methodology
- Validation framework
- Source references

---

# Research Goals

The project was designed to:

1. Identify relevant companies based on assignment criteria
2. Validate company relevance using multiple public sources
3. Evaluate growth, hiring, and business indicators
4. Create a realistic company pipeline
5. Provide structured analyst-style reasoning
6. Separate strong matches from weak or misleading candidates

---

# Anti-Hallucination Policy

This project intentionally avoids:
- fabricated funding data
- invented employee counts
- unverified revenue numbers
- unsupported partnerships
- fake hiring signals

Where data was uncertain:
- assumptions were marked clearly
- manual validation was recommended
